About

DevelopHer is the first and only full-stack development training program offered to young women in the Balkan states, specifically Kosovo. A joint initiative between two US-based organizations (Coding Dojo and Flora Stationery) and two Kosovo-based organizations (Girls Coding Kosova and Innovation Centre Kosovo), DevelopHer seeks to empower disadvantaged young women in Kosovo with technology-based skills to be competitive in the 21st century job marketplace.

The goal of the initiative is to address the nearly 60% youth unemployment rate that plagues Kosovo by creating global opportunities by empowering the young women enrolled in the program with coding skills.

Supported by

Girls Coding Kosova

Our mission is to encourage women to reach their full academic and professional potential in technical fields. Its network spans across all municipalities in Kosovo and includes over six hundred (600) active young members aged between fifteen (15) and twenty-five (25). We are constantly working on bringing events to the young and enthusiasts girls of Kosovo to encourage them to code, launch companies or work as developers in other exciting startups in the country.

Flora Stationary

Flora Stationery is a nonprofit organization which sells unique stationery products featuring original artwork from students in Kosovo to support a scholarship fund for young women pursuing a college education in Eastern Europe. The mission of Flora is to create a sustainable source of funding to support young women in need of assistance to attend a university in their home country. Through distributing a portion of the profits from every sale, young women are supported for an entire semester of college tuition.

Innovation Centre Kosova

nnovation Centre Kosovo (ICK) is a center whose aim is to connect research and development component of scientific field with the business sector, focusing on creating new job opportunities oriented towards the future, based on knowledge and new technology. Innovation Centre Kosovo (ICK) was founded to support entrepreneurship, innovation and commercially based business development, with a focus on information and communication technology. The centre supports both start-ups and existing companies with the potential for growth.
